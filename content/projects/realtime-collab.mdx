## The problem

Users wanted Google-Docs-style collaboration inside our product — multiple cursors,
instant updates, and edits that never clobber each other, even on flaky connections.

## What I built

- **CRDT-based document model** so concurrent edits merge without a central lock and work
  offline, syncing cleanly on reconnect.
- **Presence + awareness** — live cursors and selections over a WebSocket layer with
  heartbeat-based disconnect detection.
- **Durable persistence** — snapshots + operation log in PostgreSQL for point-in-time
  recovery and audit.

## Outcome

Median edit-to-visible latency landed **under 50ms** across clients, with **99.98%**
uptime over the first six months. Support tickets about "lost changes" went to zero.
